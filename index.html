Suggest Goals
Use your profile to estimate daily calories, protein, carbs, fat, water, and steps. You can still edit everything manually before saving.
How suggestions work
Calories use the Mifflin-St Jeor formula with activity multipliers from NIH/NIDDK guidance. Protein, carbs, fat, and water follow National Academies dietary reference ranges and adequate intake guidance. Steps use a simple activity-based target informed by CDC activity guidance and NIH step-count evidence.
